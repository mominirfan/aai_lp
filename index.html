Custom 4 Year Plan Creator

  • Creates the optimal four-year plan for any set of majors and minors
  • Keeps into account university curriculum and any overlaps between majors and minors
  • Applies any AP courses or credit brought into the university
  • Students can explore how their plan changes as they add more majors and minors

Program Explorer

  • Reports completion against any major or minor at university
  • Automatically analyzes student’s transcript or student’s anticipated major(s)
  • Reports the nearest major or minor across all schools at the university

Semester Planner

  • Allows the student to instantly see all the possible schedules for a set of classes for a given semester
  • Allows student to filter based on preferences
  • Can lead towards integration and beautification of clunky back-end software

Helping students graduate in less time with more accomplishments
